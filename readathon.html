Rebel Reads Book Club Presents: Read-a-Thon!

We are kicking ofd 2022 by reading in support of the EFry Ottawa Relocation Project - see below to learn more!

Join Us!

Our goal to build a space where all of the EFry Ottawa Services are located in one-stop, to provide the best support for our clients. This would mean that transitional housing, counselling services, drop-in services, long-term housing support, and Diana's Place - EFSO's family re-unification centre, will be available to all clients in one place. You can help! In honour of EFSO's 70 years in Ottawa, we're asking you to help us raise $7000 for this project. To do this, sign-up, set a reading goal, and start collecting pledges. Top fundraisers will be eligible for prizes!
